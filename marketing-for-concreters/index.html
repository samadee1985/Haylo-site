Common Questions

Questions about
trade marketing.

Can local SEO really help a concreting business?
Yes. Most concreting enquiries start with a local Google search. Businesses that appear prominently in the Maps results get the majority of those calls. Good local SEO directly impacts how much inbound work you receive.
How does Haylo target specific job types like driveways or pool surrounds?
We create targeted content on your website and optimise your Google Business Profile for specific services, helping Google show your listing to people searching for those specific job types.
What kind of photos work best for a concreter's Google Business Profile?
Finished project photos are the most valuable - driveways, patios, exposed aggregate work. Before-and-after combinations perform particularly well. We guide you on what to capture and how to use it.
How long before I see more enquiries?
Early improvements to your Google Business Profile can affect visibility within a few weeks. More sustained ranking improvements typically take 2-4 months of consistent work.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No. Haylo works month-to-month. You can pause or stop at any time with 30 days notice.
