For Health & Wellness Businesses

Your next client is
searching right now.

The Sunshine Coast has a thriving health and wellness community - physios, chiropractors, massage therapists, beauty salons, yoga studios, naturopaths and more. And when a local resident needs your services, their first move is almost always to search Google. They look at who's nearby, check the reviews, and book whoever looks most professional and trusted.

Health and wellness businesses often have excellent retention once a client is in the door - but the challenge is winning that first appointment. A strong Google Business Profile with compelling photos, accurate information, and a steady stream of five-star reviews is the most powerful tool you have for winning new patients and clients.

Haylo helps Sunshine Coast health and wellness businesses build that presence systematically - so your appointment book stays full without relying entirely on word of mouth.

Why It Matters for Health & Wellness

First impressions happen
before they call you.

When someone searches for a massage therapist or physiotherapist in their suburb, they evaluate you before they've even spoken to you. Your photos, your review count, your rating, your description - all of this builds their impression of your professionalism and care. A complete, well-optimised profile with strong reviews consistently wins that decision.

Most health and wellness practitioners on the Sunshine Coast underinvest in their Google presence. They rely on existing clients and the occasional referral. That leaves them vulnerable - and leaves a lot of new patients finding their competitors instead. Optimising your Google profile is one of the highest-return activities available for a local health business.
