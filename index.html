About Haylo

Meet Sam - the person
behind Haylo.

I started Haylo because I kept seeing the same thing: great local businesses doing excellent work, but barely showing up online. Customers were driving right past them and calling a competitor instead - simply because that competitor was easier to find on Google.

A lot of small business owners have told me: “I tried marketing once and it didn’t work.” But here’s the thing - if a barber completely butchers your hair, you don’t swear off haircuts forever. You just think: right, I’m not going back there. Then you find someone who listens. Someone who gets it. Someone you actually trust.

Marketing is the same. Most of the time it’s not that marketing doesn’t work - it’s that the approach, the strategy, or the person doing it wasn’t the right fit. Good marketing shouldn’t feel confusing or like you’re throwing money into the wind. It should feel clear, make sense, and most importantly - help the right customers find you.

I work with Sunshine Coast tradies, cleaners and service businesses to fix the basics that actually matter - your Google Business Profile, your reviews, your local visibility. Simple stuff, done properly, that brings real enquiries through the door.

No lock-in contracts. No jargon. Just honest, practical help getting your business found.
