Common Questions

Questions about
trade marketing.

Do photos on Google Business Profile really help tilers?
Yes, significantly. Tiling is a visual trade and project photos help potential clients assess your quality before they contact you. Profiles with strong photo galleries consistently outperform those without, both in ranking and conversion.
Can Haylo help me get more bathroom renovation leads specifically?
Yes. We can target specific job types - bathrooms, kitchens, pool areas, outdoor tiling - through your Google Business Profile service categories, website content, and local keyword targeting.
How do I compete with larger tiling companies on Google?
Local SEO levels the playing field. A smaller operator with a well-optimised Google Business Profile and 40+ genuine reviews will regularly outrank a larger company with a neglected profile. Consistency beats size in local search.
How long does it take to build a strong review profile?
With a systematic approach, most businesses can build a meaningful review base within 3-6 months - the key is making the process easy and consistent after every completed project.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No. Month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel.
