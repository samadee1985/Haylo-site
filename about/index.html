Why Haylo Exists

A region growing fast.
Businesses left behind.

The Sunshine Coast is one of Australia's fastest-growing regions. New suburbs are emerging from Caloundra in the south to Noosa in the north. Thousands of new residents are arriving every year - families setting up homes, people relocating from Brisbane, retirees choosing the coast. With that growth comes surging demand for every kind of local service.

Cleaners, plumbers, electricians, landscapers, builders - the businesses keeping this region functioning are busier than ever in theory. But too many of them are invisible online. When a new resident searches for a cleaner in Maroochydore or a plumber in Buderim, they find whoever has the best Google presence - not necessarily the best business.

Haylo was built to close that gap. We help local service businesses get properly visible on Google, build the kind of review reputation that wins jobs, and stay active on Facebook - so that when someone nearby searches for what you do, you're the first result they call.

Our Focus

Only local.
Only Sunshine Coast.

Haylo doesn't try to do everything for everyone. We focus exclusively on local service businesses on the Sunshine Coast - cleaners, tradies, and the services that make communities run.

That focus makes us better at what we do. We know which suburbs are growing fastest. We understand what local customers search for and how they decide who to call. We know the competition in each area and what it takes to stand out. That local knowledge is baked into every piece of work we do - and it's why our clients see results faster than they would with a generic agency.

We're not interested in big retainers, lock-in contracts, or selling services you don't need. We're interested in getting your phone ringing more often.
