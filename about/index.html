Why Haylo Exists

The Coast is growing.
Is your business keeping up?

The Sunshine Coast is one of Australia's fastest-growing regions, with thousands of new residents arriving every year. That growth creates surging demand for local services - cleaners, tradies, home services, and more.

But most service businesses are invisible online. When a new resident searches for a plumber or a cleaner, they find whoever has the best Google presence - not necessarily the best business. Haylo exists to close that gap.

We help local service businesses get visible on Google, build their review reputation, and stay active where customers are looking. No jargon, no lock-in contracts, no unnecessary complexity. Just more calls.
