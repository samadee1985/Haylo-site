Common Issues We Find

Problems costing
you enquiries.

After reviewing hundreds of local business profiles, these are the issues that come up again and again — and every one of them affects your Google Maps ranking.

Wrong primary category
The single biggest ranking factor most businesses get wrong. An incorrect primary category can drop you out of local results entirely.
Missing services or descriptions
Google uses your services list and business description to understand what you do and match you with relevant searches.
Weak business description
A vague or generic description misses the chance to include the keywords your ideal customers are searching for.
No regular Google posts
Businesses that post regularly signal to Google that their profile is active and trustworthy — most never post at all.
Low review activity
Review quantity, recency and response rate all affect your local ranking. One burst of reviews years ago is not enough.
Website not helping rankings
Your website and Google profile need to work together. Inconsistent NAP details and missing signals are leaving ranking power on the table.
