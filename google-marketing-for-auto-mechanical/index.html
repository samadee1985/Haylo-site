For Auto & Mechanical Businesses

When a car needs work,
Google gets the call.

Whether you're a local mechanic, mobile tyre fitter, detailer, or panel beater, one thing is consistent: when a Sunshine Coast driver needs help with their vehicle, they search Google first. They look at who comes up on the map, check the reviews, and call whoever looks most trustworthy.

Auto and mechanical businesses often rely on regulars and referrals - but those alone won't grow your workshop. The next generation of customers isn't asking their mate who they use. They're searching "mechanic near me" or "car service Maroochydore" and clicking whoever has the best-looking profile with the most reviews.

Haylo helps Sunshine Coast auto businesses build that profile - so you're the one getting found, getting called, and getting the job.

Why It Matters for Auto & Mechanical

Reviews and visibility win
workshop bookings.

Car owners feel vulnerable when their vehicle needs work - they don't know what's wrong, and they need to trust who they hand the keys to. That's why reviews matter so much in this industry. A mechanic with 80 five-star reviews beats a larger workshop with 12 mixed reviews every time.

Most auto businesses on the Sunshine Coast are not actively managing their Google presence. That creates a real opportunity - the mechanic who gets to the top 3 on Google Maps in their suburb and builds a strong review profile consistently outperforms competitors with similar or better service.
