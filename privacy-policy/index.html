Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Who we are

Haylo is a local marketing business based on the Sunshine Coast, Queensland, helping small service businesses get found online. You can reach us at hello@withhaylo.com or on 0490 422 192.

What information we collect

When you submit an enquiry or contact us through this website, we may collect your name, business name, email address, phone number, and any business details you choose to share. All information is provided voluntarily - we don't collect anything without you actively submitting it.

How we use your information

Information you submit is used solely to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to deliver the services you've engaged us for. We don't sell your information, share it with third parties for marketing purposes, or add you to mailing lists without your consent.

How we store your information

Enquiries submitted through our website are processed via Formspree, a secure form submission service. Contact details for clients and prospective clients are stored in our business records for the purpose of managing our working relationship.

Cookies and analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This may involve the use of cookies to collect anonymised data such as pages visited and time on site. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics using your browser settings or a browser extension.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, and to request that we correct or delete it. To make a request, email us at hello@withhaylo.com.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Get in touch: hello@withhaylo.com or 0490 422 192.
