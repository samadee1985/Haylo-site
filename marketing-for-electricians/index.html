Common Questions

How quickly can local SEO improve my Google ranking?
It varies depending on competition and your starting point. Some improvements can have an effect within weeks. Sustained ranking improvements typically take 2-4 months of consistent work.
Do reviews really make a difference for electricians?
Yes, significantly. Google uses review quantity and rating as a ranking factor. Beyond that, most homeowners check reviews before calling - a strong review profile builds the trust needed to convert a searcher into a caller.
Can Haylo help if I service multiple areas?
Absolutely. We can optimise your profile and website for multiple service areas across the Sunshine Coast and build location-specific content for each area you cover.
What's included in the monthly reporting?
You'll receive a clear report covering Google Business Profile performance (calls, views, searches), review progress, citation work completed, and notes on what we're working on next.

No. We work month-to-month. If you want to pause or cancel, just give us 30 days notice.
