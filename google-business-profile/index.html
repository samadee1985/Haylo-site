Common Questions

Google Business Profile
FAQs.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients see meaningful ranking improvements within 2-6 weeks. Some see changes within days of optimisation. The timeline depends on how competitive your local market is and how much work your profile needs. We'll give you a realistic expectation after your free audit.

Do I need to do anything, or is it all handled for you?

Mostly hands-off for you. We handle the setup, writing, posting, and ongoing management. The main thing we need from you is access to your profile, some photos of your work, and occasionally a heads-up if something changes in your business (new services, updated hours, etc.).

I already have a Google Business Profile - do I still need this?

Almost certainly yes. Having a profile and having an optimised profile are very different things. In our experience, 9 out of 10 profiles we audit are missing critical information, have the wrong categories set, or haven't been updated in months. All of these things hurt your ranking. We'll show you exactly what yours is missing in your free audit.

What's the difference between Google Business Profile and Google Ads?

Google Business Profile optimisation earns you organic (free) placement in Google Maps. Google Ads are paid listings that appear above the map. We focus on organic ranking because the results are sustainable - you're not paying per click, and a well-ranked profile keeps working for you 24/7 without ongoing ad spend.

Can you work with any type of local service business?

Yes. We work with cleaners, tradies, lawn and garden services, home services, mechanics, health and wellness businesses, and any other local service provider on the Sunshine Coast. If your customers find you by searching Google, we can help.
