Common Questions

Questions about
trade marketing.

What trades does Haylo work with?
We work with all residential and commercial service trades on the Sunshine Coast - plumbers, electricians, roofers, tilers, concreters, painters, carpenters, landscapers, cleaners, and more. If your customers search Google to find you, we can help.
Why is Google Maps more important than a website for tradies?
For local service searches, the Google Maps pack appears above all organic website results and captures the majority of clicks. A strong Maps presence often delivers more enquiries than a well-ranked website alone. Both work better together, but Maps is typically the higher priority starting point.
How does Haylo differ from other marketing agencies?
We focus exclusively on local marketing for small trade businesses. We understand the specific things that affect local search rankings and we don't upsell services that aren't relevant to your goals.
How quickly can I expect to see an improvement?
Some improvements can have an effect within weeks. Building sustained ranking improvements typically takes 2-4 months of consistent work. We set realistic expectations from the start.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No. Haylo works month-to-month. You can pause or cancel with 30 days notice.
